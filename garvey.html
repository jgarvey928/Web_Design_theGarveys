- Garvey History -

The following section is devoted to a history of the Garvey lineage. This page follows the male lineage of the name Garvey that leads all the way to me. The trail starts at the last generation to live in Ireland being Michael. Michael along with his two children Nancy and William left Ireland for America around 1860. William was nearly thirty years old by the time he came to America and was immediately drafted into the civil war. William survives and goes on to marry Mary Casey and together they had five children which were the first to be born in America. All of whom are buried at Saint John's Cemetery in Worcester, Massachusetts and can be found at findagrave.com

.
Michael Garvey

Michael Garvey was born in Ireland around 1805 and had three children with a one Ellen Dunn. While living in Ireland Ellen gave birth to Nancy, Ellen and then William all who immigrated to America with Michael around 1860. However, Ellen did not she most likely died in Ireland.

  • Born: abt 1805, Ireland
  • Residence: Ireland / Middlesex,Massachusetts
  • Spouse: Ellen Dunn
  • Children: Nancy, Ellen, William*
  • Death: unknown
William Garvey

William Garvey was born in Ireland in 1835. Later with his father and sister Nancy, William immigrated to America around the age of 30. Apon arriving in America William was immediately drafed into the Civil War. He served with the 4th Battery of the Massachusetts Light Artillery. After the war William married Mary Casey and together they had five children Mary, Agnes, John Henry, Johanna, and Theresa. William died and was buried at the Saint John's Cemetery in Worcester, Massachusetts in 1911 at the age of 76.

  • Born: 1835, Ireland
  • Residence: Ireland / Springflied,Massachusetts / Worcester,Massachusetts
  • Spouse: Mary A. Garvey
  • Children: Mary, Agnes, John Henry*, Johanna, Theresa
  • Military: Civil War
  • Death: 1911 (age 76)
John Henry Garvey

John Henry Garvey was born in Worcester, Massachusetts in 1872. Later John would marry Helen and together they would have one child John Garvey in 1922. John Henry died in his hometown in 1956 and was buried with his father, mother and four siblings in the Saint John's Cemetery in Worcester, Massachusetts.

  • Born: 1872, Worcester, Massachusetts
  • Residence: Worcester,Massachusetts
  • Spouse: Helen Garvey
  • Children: John*
  • Death: 1956 (age 84)
John Garvey

John Garvey was born in Worcester, Massachusetts in 1922. Later John would marry Ruth Long and together they would have three children Diane, Daniel, and Dennis. John was killed in a car accident by a drunk driver at the age of 43.

  • Born:1922, Worcester, Massachusetts

  • Spouse: Ruth Long

Dennis Garvey

Dennis Garvey was born in Springflied, Massachusetts on May 4, 1955. Dennis would marry Geralyn Studley in 1980. Together they had two children Maggie and John. Later in 2002, Dennis and his family would move to Prescott, Arizona where he became a dean of gerontology at Yavapai Community College.

  • Born:May 4, 1955, Springflied,Massachusetts
