- Brief Family History -

The Garvey family came from Ireland and has lived in Massachusetts since around 1860. Michael and his two children Nancy and William were the first generation to immegrate to America. William was nearly thirty years old when he arrived and was immediately drafed into the civil war and serverd in the 4th Battery of the Massachusetts Light Artillery. Later on, William's grandson and my gradfather John would marry Ruth Long in Springflied, Massachusetts and have two sons and one daughter. In the early 1980s, Dennis son of John and Ruth, my father, meets Geralyn Studley, daughter of Merl and Dorothy, at Cape Code Community College. Dennis and Gerry had two kids a daughter Maggie and a son John Garvey, me.

- Goals -

The goal of this website is to create an virtual archive for the history of the Garvey family. First, to trace the lineage of the surname Garvey that led to my birth. Next to illustrate the Garvey lineage in a family tree. Finally, to archive old photos of my life and then even older photos of the family going back several generations.
