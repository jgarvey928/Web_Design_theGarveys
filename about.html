- About -

"How We Got to Me" is a virtual archive containing photos and documentation regarding the Garvey family. This website contains the following ...

Home
A brief history of the Garvey family surname. As well as a summary of the goals of this website.
Garvey
This page contains a historical trace of the lineage of the Garvey surname that led to myself. The trail begins five generations back with the oldest Garvey that moved from Ireland to America Michael. Each generation has a breif historical account of their lives. The trail ends with myself.
Family Tree
This page contains an illustration of the Garvey lineage in the form of a family tree. The bottom of the tree starts with the oldest generations born in Ireland. Then the top of the tree branches out into the newest generations of Garveys.
DNA Report
This page contains a pie chart desribing my own genetic makeup. The chart shows the approximate proportions of my own dna that can be traced to different regions in the world. Also a table that breaksdown how much each region is inherited from either my father or mother.
My Photos
This page contains multiple photos of me thoughout the years. Each photo can be clicked on the enlarge. The photos are arranged in order from oldest to newest. There are forms that when submitted will narrow down the number of photos the page shows.
Family Photos
The last page contains multiple photos of the family thoughout the years. Each photo can be clicked on the enlarge. The photos are arranged in order from oldest to newest. There are forms that when submitted will narrow down the number of photos the page shows.
